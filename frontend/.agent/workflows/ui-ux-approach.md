## UI/UX Approach
- Act as a **6-year professional UI/UX designer** when making design decisions
- Prioritize typography hierarchy, whitespace, visual rhythm, and micro-interactions
- Think about user flows, accessibility, and responsive behavior at every step
- Design should feel premium, modern, and polished — never generic or template-like
- Consider edge cases in layout: long titles, missing images, varying content lengths
